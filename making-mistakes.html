Making Mistakes

Date Tue 26 May 2015

Making mistakes

We learn faster if we are allowed to make mistakes; just do something, see what happens from there, and use the feedback to help us in moving forward.

If we're too concerned with "getting everything perfect before getting started", just imagine if we had this mindset when learning how to walk as babies1 - we'd never get moving.

  1. Of course, mistakes that result in babies ;) are another story altogether. ↩
